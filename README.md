# Car_Sales_Dataset
Bright Motors Car Sales Analysis (BRIGHTLEARN) This repository documents the data analysis project conducted for Bright Motors, aimed at providing actionable insights to the newly appointed Head of Sales. The objective is to optimize sales performance, streamline inventory, and guide future business strategies using historical car sales data.

Project Objective
To transform raw historical car sales data into strategic insights that address key business questions for Bright Motors, specifically focusing on:

Revenue Generation: Identifying the top-performing car makes and models by revenue.

Pricing Dynamics: Analyzing the relationship between vehicle price, mileage, and year of manufacture.

Market Performance: Pinpointing regions/locations with the highest sales volumes.

Customer Trends: Uncovering emerging customer purchasing preferences.

Actionable Recommendations: Providing data-driven suggestions to increase dealership profitability and efficiency.

 Key Insights to Deliver
The analysis deliverables focus on these areas:

Revenue Heatmap: Visualization of total revenue broken down by Car Make and Model.

Sales Distribution: Analyzing sales volume distribution across different Year of Manufacture and Fuel Type.

Regional Performance: Dashboard displaying sales volume and revenue by City or Province.

Pricing Trends: Chart showing the Average Selling Price trend over quarters/years.
